Why Work With Me

In addition to firmly believing in principles like the Occam's Razor, DRY, or KISS:
Continuous Shifting Between Bigger Picture and Detailed Implementation

The development Activity must remain Customer-centric.
An adequate product will only be obtained by alternating between the Bigger Picture and the Detailed Implementation.

This also helps with Validation and Verification ensuring that the product meets its requirements.

OOP, SOLID and FP principles for Efficiency, Maintainability and Speed

Code is not written for machines but for humans, 80% of the time code is looked at is after development is done.

This calls for Software that complies with good practices allowing maintainability, modularity, scalability, readability, and efficiency.

Product Confidence Through Integrated Testing

The current context of Software Development promotes Short and consecutive Iterations of development. This enables many advantages such as faster feedback, for example.

Integrated Testing throughout the SLDC will certainly help QA build Product Confidence.

Communication and Teamwork for Speed and Quality

Communication is the cornerstone of every team especially in the context of Software Development.

Effective communication with the diverse shareholders and within the team will enable Efficiency and Quality.
